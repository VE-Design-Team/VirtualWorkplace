Scenario Information

You are a personal care worker at Scottish Thistle residential estate, an aged care facility.

This is what was handed to you at the start of your shift on the 6 April.

Handover notes

“Dot complained of burning pain upon urination and aching lower back pain this morning; staff also noted that she had been incontinent of urine on 3 separate occasions in the past 24 hours.

She has been refusing to eat or drink due to feeling generally unwell, so she has been commenced on continence, food, fluid and pain charts to monitor her condition over this period. Staff are to encourage her to drink plenty of sugar-free fluids during this time.

Staff are to collect a urine sample for analysis and she has been referred to her GP for further assessment. Staff continue to monitor.”

Throughout your shift, you monitored Dot’s food intake. Read the following information carefully as you will have to input this information on the next page into the 'Food and fluids' record.

  • 8:00 Breakfast - Two slices of toast and a glass of juice which she was able to eat and drink completely, this took her about 15 minutes.
  • 9:00 After breakfast - She felt a little unwell and did not drink any of her tea.
  • 10:30 Morning tea - She was able to drink a whole glass of water (100ml) by taking several sips over 10 minutes.
  • 12:00 Lunch - She took 20 minutes but could only eat half of her sandwich and half a cup of coffee (125ml).

On the next page you will find the 'Food and fluids' record.

The details from the previous two days when the 'Food and fluids' record began are already in place in the form. You can use this information as a guide to assist you with the details you need to record.

It is important this document is accurate as it could be used by medical staff to highlight a health issue.

All the blank spaces need to be filled; some will need a text input and others will have a drop-down menu which can be accessed by selecting the small black triangle on the right-hand side of the blank space.

Once you have familiarised yourself with the date, times, food and fluid items, and the amounts taken, select the Next button to continue.
